About Us

Based in North Carolina, we have over 20 years of dive experience. We are a PADI instructor couple who offer training and dive guide services. We are a part time company, running this business on the side, so we know what it is like to be flexible. We can work around any schedule to help you meet your goals or get in some new experiences.

We offer tailored training plans and dive guide services in the NC area. Whatever your goal, recreation or techincal diving, we can get you there. We have an extensive network of boat captains and technical instructors with whom we out-source and contract with

If traveling to and diving in the 7 seas is what your after, join us on one of our international dive trips. We are vast community all around the world, traveling in groups to places like, Greece, Belize, Jordan..etc. We offer group rates and handle all the details and planning.

We dive year round and organize many events like lionfish hunts, underwater pumpkin carving, scavanger hunts, night dives...etc. This year we are looking forward to working with other local dive shops in the area to attempt to break the Guiness World Record for the largest underwater wedding.
